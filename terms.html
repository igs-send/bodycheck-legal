About the app

Body Check is a personal health-tracking tool. It lets you record how you feel on a visual body map, keep a health diary, take self-check tests, and prepare a summary to share with your doctor.

Body Check is not a medical device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, prevent, or monitor any disease or medical condition, and it does not provide medical advice. The “Doctor Summary” feature simply gathers your own notes into a single text so you can copy and share it — it is not a medical assessment and contains no automated diagnosis. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.
