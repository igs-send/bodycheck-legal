Body Check
Health diary & body map

Your health, mapped.

Body Check is a private, offline health diary built around a visual body map. Note how you feel and see it right on your body — no account needed.

Download on the App Store Get it on Google Play

What you can do

Visual body map

Tap any area of the body to log what's going on, right where you feel it.

Symptom & mood diary

Track symptoms, pain and mood over time, with notes in your own words.

Teeth tracking

Keep a dental map — cavities, care and notes for each tooth.

Self-tests

Built-in questionnaires to check in on how you're doing and track it over time.

Health budget

Log what you spend on your health and keep it all in one place.

Notes & files

Attach photos and documents to your entries so everything is together.

Clear summaries

Generate a clear summary of your history before you visit a specialist.

Reminders & streaks

Gentle reminders, streaks and rewards help you make it a daily habit.

Private by design Your entries stay on your device. We run no servers of our own and never receive your health data. Back up privately to your own Google Drive, or export a local file — you're always in control.

Free to use

Body Check is free to download and use. Some advanced features — such as extra file attachments, clear doctor summaries, full teeth history, the health budget and cloud backup — are part of an optional premium.

Body Check is a personal health-tracking tool. It is not a medical device, does not diagnose conditions, and does not replace professional medical advice.
